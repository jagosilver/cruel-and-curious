# Stowe Barton

Stowe Barton is an 18th-century stone barn complex on a [National Trust](https://www.nationaltrust.org.uk) property near the village of Stibb in North Cornwall. It overlooks Duckpool beach and sits within the parish of Morwenstow — once presided over by the legendary Reverend Hawker.

**Coordinates:** 50°52'25.46"N, 4°32'26.04"W  
**Nearest village:** Stibb  
**Coast:** Duckpool beach, a short walk away  
**Address:** Near Bude, Cornwall, EX23 9JW

## Exhibition spaces

The barn complex has three distinct exhibition areas used by Cruel & Curious:

- **The Shippin** — the main ground-floor barn space
- **The Shed** — an adjacent outbuilding
- **The Loft** — an upper-level gallery space, added for [[Sea II (2014)]]

## Setting

The location is central to the exhibition's identity. The brief has always described art that makes visitors "a little freaked out about the sea, which lies just a few hundred feet away." The Duckpool beach below the barns — wild, exposed, surf-hammered — provides that context directly.
