# Cruel & Curious: Heart — 2022

| | |
|--|--|
| **Title** | Cruel & Curious: Heart |
| **Year** | 2022 |
| **Theme** | The heart |
| **Venue** | [[Stowe Barton]], North Cornwall |
| **Dates** | 23–24 September 2022 |
| **Times** | Fri 5:00–9:30 pm · Sat 3:00–9:30 pm |
| **Artists** | 35+ |
| **Performance** | James Dixon (Saturday 1:30–3:00 pm) |
| **Coffee** | Maggie's Mobile Barista |

## Notes

The 2020 and 2021 editions were cancelled due to the pandemic. Heart was the first exhibition since [[Nocturne (2019)]].
