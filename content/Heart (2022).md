## Notes

The 2020 and 2021 editions were likely cancelled due to the pandemic. Heart was the first exhibition since [[Nocturne (2019)]].

Jago produced a related print series — *Cruel & Curious Heart* — with large-format prints mounted on birch ply from The Printspace.
