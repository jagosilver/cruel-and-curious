## Brief

The Nocturne theme extended the exhibition's uncanny atmosphere into darkness — figures moving through night landscapes unaware of what surrounds them.

Jago's work for this show was a series of black-and-white illustrations of a traveller unaware of creatures and environments lurking in the dark, inspired by Jeffrey Alan Love's *Notes From The Shadowed City*. Planned as small A5 books alongside larger prints.

The **Nocturne Whale & Swimmer** print that emerged from this exhibition became the highest-grossing single product in the Jago Illustration shop: 261 units sold, £6,036 revenue.
