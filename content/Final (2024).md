# Cruel & Curious Sea — Final Edition, 2024

| | |
|--|--|
| **Title** | Cruel & Curious Sea (Final) |

| **Theme** | The sea — 10th anniversary |
| **Venue** | [[Stowe Barton]], North Cornwall |
| **Dates** | September 2024 |

## Notes

The last ever Cruel & Curious exhibition. The 10th anniversary edition closed out a run that began informally in [[Sea I (2013)|2013]] and formally in 2014.

A **10th Anniversary Reissue** calendar was also released to mark the occasion (178 units sold through the Jago Illustration shop).
