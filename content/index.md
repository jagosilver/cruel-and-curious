# Cruel & Curious

An annual art and illustration exhibition held at [[Stowe Barton]] — an 18th-century stone barn on National Trust land near Bude, North Cornwall. Free entry. 25–35 artists. One weekend in September.

Running since 2013.

> *"Anti-postcard, unsettlingly gloomy… think heavy winter seas and dagger-edged cliffs, malevolent swells, beachside oddities, tales of mermaids and monsters. The idea that people walk out of the show a little freaked out about the sea, which lies just a few hundred feet away."*

## About the venue

[[Stowe Barton]] is a historic farmstead on the North Cornwall coast, managed by the National Trust. The exhibition occupies three spaces: The Shippin, The Shed, and The Loft.
