# Cruel & Curious Sea I — 2013

The first Cruel & Curious exhibition, held at [[Stowe Barton]], September 2013.

| | |
|--|--|
| **Title** | Cruel & Curious Sea I |
| **Year** | 2013 |
| **Theme** | The sea — myth, wreck, storm |
