# Cruel & Curious Sea I — 2013

The first Cruel & Curious exhibition. An informal debut at [[Stowe Barton]], autumn 2013.

|--|--|
| **Title** | Cruel & Curious Sea I |

## Notes

The first edition was informal and small-scale. The artist brief for the second show refers back to "the success of the first event last autumn," confirming this took place in 2013. The Loft gallery space had not yet been added (that came with [[Sea II (2014)|Sea II]]).
